### About Me

I'm a high school student from Netanya, Israel, who got into programming through curiosity —
and stayed because building things that actually work is genuinely satisfying.

Most of my projects sit somewhere between systems and product: low-level enough to be interesting,
useful enough to matter. I care about understanding how things work under the hood —
not just making them run.

Right now I'm focused on reverse engineering, networking, and full-stack tools.
I like projects with a clear purpose: something that teaches, inspects, visualizes, or automates.

Always trying to write better code than I did last month.
